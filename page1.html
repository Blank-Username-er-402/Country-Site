France is the most visited country in the world!

France is a massively visited country due to the amount of tourist attractions it has.
It is known for large tourist attractons such as the Eiffel Tower as well as many smaller
landmarks and even castles. Food and culture are main attraction factors for tourists as
France is renowned for its food as well as defined culture and art. France has long been
considered an artisitic hotspot and museums such as the Louvre attract hundreds of thousands
of people each year.
