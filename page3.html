France is smaller than texas.

France while being a cultural hotspot and tourist attraction is only, in fact, about the size of Texas.
In fact its even a little smaller at 1.2 times smaller than Texas it would only make up about 81.34% of
Texas. Even while being smaller than a U.S state Frances geography is a patchwork of beauty, ranging from
warm sunkissed shores on the Mediterenean coast to tall and snowy mountains and valleys that many skiers
call home. You cant forget the sprawling metropolis of Paris dotted with museums and and pieces of history
seemingly dragged into a modern age. The geography is truly incredible with forests so unique you would
believe you went back into the past in a fantasy world. In many parts of France there are snapshots of the past:
ancient castles dating back as far as 850 AD.
